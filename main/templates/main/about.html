Who are we


Eurofix is a leading industrial construction materials manufacturer offering a complete range of solutions specifically developed using advanced formulation technology to meet the highest technical requirements.


Euroﬁx industries was founded in Istanbul, Turkey in 1997. Since then it has expanded its employing operation to Kenya employing 243 people. Euroﬁx primary area of expertise includes solutions for laying tiles, waterprooﬁng, interior and exterior ﬁnishing products, ready-to-use cement, bricks, cabro (pavement blocks) and other construction materials.


In Kenya, we have manufacturing facilities in Nairobi and Mombasa and distributors located in strategic locations enabling us to provide a highly responsive and quick service to our customers. At Euroﬁx, we manufacture all our products with the same high quality and ensure a consistent high performance in conformance with International Quality Standards. As a result, Eurofix is well appreciated as a symbol of quality and reliability


We also believe passionately in taking care of our environment and combating global warming, hence we are on a campaign to plant over 500,000 trees in Kenya, as well as working towards becoming a net-zero company.
