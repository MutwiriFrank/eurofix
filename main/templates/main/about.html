Who are we


Eurofix is a leading industrial construction materials manufacturer offering a complete range of solutions specifically developed using advanced formulation technology to meet the highest technical requirements.


Euroﬁx industries was founded in Istanbul, Turkey in 1997. Since then it has expanded its employing operation to Kenya employing 243 people. Euroﬁx primary area of expertise includes solutions for laying tiles, waterprooﬁng, interior and exterior ﬁnishing product, ready to use cement for, bricks, cabro and other construction materials.


In kenya we have a manufacturing facilities in Nairobi and Mombasa and distributors located in strategic locations enabling us to provide a highly responsive and quick service to our customers. Euroﬁx manufactures all our products with same high quality and ensure a consistent highperfomance in comformance with International Quality Standards. As a result Eurofix is well appreciated as a symbol of quality and reliability.


We are committed to better and brighter lives for communities we operate in, and the nation at large, by sponsoring free fundis and youth training national wide with a aim of training over 6000 youths and upgrading skills of over 3000 fundis, through our Corporate Social Responsibility investments. We believe passionately in taking care of our environment and combating global warming, hence we are on campagn to plant over 500,000 trees in kenya, as well as working towards becoming a net zero company.
